If you have a modern browser with ServiceWorker support, some of these will be served from cache the next time. Try reloading the page, and experiment what happens when the network is offline. In Chrome:

  • Switch to "Network" tab
  • Turn on "Offline" and "Disable cache"
  • Reload this page.
